## What Is VR Sailing?

<!--more-->

VR sailing **recreates real-world sailing in a fully immersive 3D environment**. Slip on a lightweight, wireless headset and find yourself at the helm of a virtual boat.

Modern physics engines‚Äîeven showing details like **prop walk** in reverse‚Äîmake the experience feel authentic. Think of it like the flight simulators airline pilots use: more than a game, it‚Äôs genuine training.

1. **Study Theory:** Books, videos, or online courses.

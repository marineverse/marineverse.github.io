Today, we are chatting with **Filippo Ferrante**, an Italian based in Leeds, UK.

<!--more-->

In virtual reality, you can meet Filippo in MarineVerse Sailing Club as [Filippo](https://www.marineverse.com/members/NinUEeikpwVj0e2fV-kPJMjoULOhWAUtDnx7rAH8X7RYfIU) (sail number 25142). Filippo races in Yacht Pro with a current DRP rating of 1425 and is currently ranked #184 in the [Yacht Pro league](https://www.marineverse.com/marineverse-cup/rating/pro).

Filippo is also sailing in [Globe](https://www.marineverse.com/globe/boats-profiles/783b7b83-8160-40ac-bb14-6f3d0e2c6f7b), currently near Sydney!

<img src="/assets/community/fillipo/Filippo2.jpg" style="max-width: 600px; width: 100%; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; display: block;"/>

We have asked Filippo a couple of questions..


**What is your sailing experience?**

*Never sailed for real apart from the odd rowing boat. I've always been looking for a real sailing game and it doesn't get any better than MarineVerse.*

**What is your favourite sailing memory?**

*Watching the America's Cup.*

**Tell us something about your experience with MarineVerse Sailing Club? Any good memories or highlights?**

*Cannot get any closer to sailing if not through MarineVerse. Brilliant game. Great to challenge everyday same sailors from around the world.*

**Who and why should try MarineVerse Sailing Club?**

*Anybody that has a passion for sailing even if you've never sailed before!*

**Any tips for those new to sailing that want to race in MarineVerse?**

*Try all the different boats and techniques. I'm stuck and love sailing the yacht.*

**Anything else you'd like to share?**

*Would love to see the multiplayer aspect of the game grow.*

## Why sail Globe?

It is a surprisingly fun way to practice the critical mental aspects of ocean racing from the safety of your home:

* **Weather Routing:** You are sailing in real-time weather patterns. Timing a system versus getting parked in a high-pressure ridge makes all the difference.
* **Watch Keeping:** There is no autopilot for navigation hazards. Your boat sails while you sleep, but it is *up to you* to check in and ensure you don't hit land.

## A new experiment: Visualizing the Voyage

We want Globe to be more than just a leaderboard; we want it to be a canvas for digital storytelling. Track on a map tells where you went, but it doesn't capture what it felt like. We want to give you the tools to illustrate your own adventure, transforming raw race data into beautiful visuals.

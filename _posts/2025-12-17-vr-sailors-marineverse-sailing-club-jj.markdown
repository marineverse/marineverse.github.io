Ahoy!

Welcome to the twenty fifth post in the series **"VR Sailors - MarineVerse Community"** – introducing sailors racing in [MarineVerse Sailing Club](https://www.marineverse.com/marineverse-cup).

<img src="/assets/globe/valpariso.jpg" style="max-width: 700px; width: 100%; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; display: block;"/>

Today, we are chatting with **JJ**, from Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

<!--more-->

[JJ](https://www.marineverse.com/members/uHbh11tdQEiRLOESzGKlNANgOec8cVOp6rgKejlu9hbN4ls) races in [Yacht Pro](https://www.marineverse.com/marineverse-cup/rating/pro/americas/northern-america/country-CA) and is currently #4 in Canada (as of 17 December 2025) and in Globe sailing his cruiser [Canada XXVII](https://www.marineverse.com/globe/boats-profiles/fdf59dbb-bb25-4561-8ac1-1797cf96b999).

JJ recently won the [SF to Valparaiso Globe race](https://www.marineverse.com/globe/races/ac5e2106-8600-4c85-b609-3cc340a29deb)!

We have asked JJ a couple of questions..


**What is your sailing experience?**
*"Learning how to sail (late start)."*

**What is your favourite sailing memory?**
*"Being on the water during the 37th America's Cup final in Barcelona and having the opportunity to sail on the double America's Cup winning boat Intrepid in Newport."*

<img src="/assets/community/jj1.jpg" style="max-width: 700px; width: 100%; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; display: block;"/>

**Tell us something about your experience with MarineVerse Sailing Club? Any good memories or highlights?**
*"I'm enjoying seeing myself get better (slowly)."*

**Who and why should try MarineVerse Sailing?**
*"Anyone interested in sailing or looking to put theory into practice."*

**Any tips for those new to sailing that want to race in MarineVerse?**
*"Don't get discouraged racing in the daily races… it takes some time but you'll see improvements."*
